The Best Movies According to Dias

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Shawshank Redemption

I like this movie because it shows the fragility of human and the friendship you form with others.

Spider-Man: Across The Spider-verse

This movie has great visuals and groundbreaking animation style. I like the storyline and can't wait for the final movie.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

This movie opens up multiversal Spider-Man in the MCU. I like this movie cause it resets MCU's Spider-Man, to be his own man, and build himself from zero.
